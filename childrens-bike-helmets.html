Safe and comfortable children’s bike helmets

Our children’s bike helmets are focused on safety and comfort. The helmet’s in-mold construction provides excellent shock absorption, while the fit is specifically engineered to protect the crucial parts of the head. The inside is lined with mesh for a soft and comfortable feeling. Mesh fabric also allows for ventilation while insects and dirt are kept outside. A safe and easy click system at the chin makes opening and closing a cinch. The back of the helmets feature an integrated light for improved visibility in the dark. All Urban Iki bicycle helmets meet the high quality requirements of the European standard EN 1078.

Fitting bike helmets for children in different age groups

Urban Iki cycling helmets are available in two sizes: XS and S. Size XS is suitable for children with a head circumference of 44 to 48 centimeters. This means the Urban Iki XS helmet is a great baby bike helmet. Thanks to an easy adjustable fit, our helmets grow along with your child. Urban Iki bike helmets in size S are made for children with a head circumference of 48 to 52 cm, and are thus a great choice for children up to around 4 years old.
