Complete your child’s bike seat with smart accessories

Accessories are like ice-cream on a sunny day, they simply make things better! Our child bike seat accessories allow you to upgrade your seat’s comfort, style and ease of use. Choose the addition that suits your needs and complete your Urban Iki child’s bike seat!

Shield your little one from the elements with an Urban Iki windscreen

Urban Iki windscreens help you to enjoy every day, no matter the weather. Our screens have an elegant, slim design, and are available in various colors to match your front seat. But a windscreen not only adds to the look, it also shields your little one from wind, bugs, sand and dust. The tinted screens reduce shimmer and protects your child’s eyes. The Urban Iki windscreen is easily mounted on the mounting block of your Urban Iki front seat. To give your child something to hold on to, you can combine the screen with our bike seat handlebar.
